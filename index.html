How Does it Work?

Explore LA and share your love of food with awesome people.

Los Angeles is home to an endless variety of cuisines, talented chefs, and creative restauranteurs that continue to make this city a top food attraction. As members, you'll have the chance to explore the best food this city has to offer. Best of all, you'll join a tigh-knit community of 'foodies' to venture out with.

Get your grub on! The Supper Club organizes multiple dinners throughout the semester. We pick a diverse set of LA restaurants for people to choose from.


Hungry?

There's a whole food scene out there to explore. We aim to find LA's best and trendiest food spots to satisfy our taste buds. All we ask is you come hungry.

Diversity & Inclusion

Regardless of where you're from, we can all relate to food. Our aim is to be an inclusive organization that encourages diversity by our willingness to constantly try different cuisines and learn from other cultures.

Community

One of the biggest aims of the Supper Club is to create a community of passionate foodies. Every event and activity is geared towards building this community. Get to know your city and meet new people.
